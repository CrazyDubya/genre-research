## Plot Outline

### Act I: The Setup (25%)

**Opening:**
- Establish hero's normal
- Establish heroine's normal
- Show what's missing in each

**The Meet Cute:**
- Scene description:
- Dynamic established:
- Initial impression:

**Inciting Incident:**
- Catalyst that brings them together/throws them together:
- Can't avoid each other:

**Early Development:**
- Forced proximity or repeated interactions:
- Attraction acknowledged (by reader if not characters):
- Conflicts established:

**End of Act I:**
- Commitment to the journey
- Stakes established

### Act II: The Development (50%)

**First Half:**
- Bonding moments
- Chemistry building
- Conflicts and complications
- Getting to know each other

**Midpoint:**
- Major shift or revelation
- Stakes raised
- Something changes fundamentally

**Second Half:**
- Intimacy increases (emotional/physical)
- Obstacles multiply
- Testing and proving
- Growing feelings

**Black Moment:**
- All seems lost
- Relationship appears over
- Must choose: love or fear?

### Act III: The Resolution (25%)

**The Turn:**
- Realization or grand gesture
- Walls come down
- Love acknowledged

**The Climax:**
- Final obstacle overcome
- Growth demonstrated
- Relationship confirmed

**The HEA:**
- Commitment made clear
- Future hopeful
- Reader satisfied
