## Beta Reader Feedback

### Questions to Ask:

1. Did you believe they were attracted to each other? When?
2. Did you feel the chemistry/tension?
3. Were there any boring parts?
4. Any parts confusing?
5. Did the HEA feel earned?
6. Was the pacing right?
7. What was your favorite moment?
8. What was your least favorite?
9. Did you root for them to be together?
10. Would you read more by this author?

### Common Issues to Watch:

- "They fell in love too fast" → Pacing issue, build foundation
- "I didn't feel the chemistry" → Show, don't tell attraction
- "The conflict felt fake" → Root in character, make believable
- "I got bored in the middle" → Pacing, add complications
- "The ending was rushed" → Build to resolution more gradually
