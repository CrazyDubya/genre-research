**Two Non-Negotiables:**
‚úÖ Central love story is the MAIN plot
‚úÖ HEA or HFN ending required

**Character Growth:**
‚úÖ Both characters must grow through love
‚úÖ Hero becomes worthy of heroine
‚úê Heroine claims her happiness
‚úÖ Better together than apart

### Chemistry and Tension

‚úÖ Emotional connection built

‚úÖ Intimacy progresses naturally
‚úÖ Push and pull dynamic

### Conflict Requirements

‚úÖ Internal conflicts (fears and flaws)

‚úÖ Romantic complications

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Always start with character** - Know who they are, what they fear, what they need
2. **Map the relationship arc** - From strangers to HEA, what happens?
3. **Build chemistry deliberately** - Don't just tell, show attraction
4. **Create real obstacles** - Conflicts that force growth
5. **Earn the payoff** - HEA must be won through character growth
