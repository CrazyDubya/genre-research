## Plot Outline

### Act I: Setup

**Opening Scene:**
- Establish status quo
- Introduce protagonist in their world
- Set comic tone
- Hook audience

**Inciting Incident:**
- Problem/challenge emerges
- Sets story in motion
- Creates comic situation

**First Complications:**
- Early attempts to solve problem
- Backfire hilariously
- Establish character flaws
- Comic situations emerge

### Act II: The Journey

**First Half:**
- Protagonist tries to solve problem
- Each attempt creates new problems
- Complications multiply
- Character flaws get in the way

**Midpoint:**
- Major escalation
- Stakes raised
- Character's worst fears
- All seems lost

**Second Half:**
- Desperate attempts
- Escalating chaos
- Rock bottom
- Character must confront truth

### Act III: Resolution

**The Turn:**
- Character has realization
- Growth moment
- New approach
- Confronts flaw/truth

**The Climax:**
- Comic payoff
- Problem solved hilariously
- Maximum absurdity
- Chaos then order

**Resolution:**
- New normal established
- Character changed
- Comic closure
- Final laugh
