## Clue Complexity Level

**Simple Clues (Surface level):**
- Easily understood
- Direct meaning
- Obvious importance

**Medium Clues (Require thought):**
- Need interpretation
- Connect to other information
- Not immediately obvious

**Complex Clues (Require expertise):**
- Technical knowledge
- Expert interpretation
- Specialist analysis

**Balance:**
- Too simple = boring
- Too complex = frustrating
- Aim for mix with progression
