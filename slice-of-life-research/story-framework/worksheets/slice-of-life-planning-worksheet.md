## Part 9: Revision Checklist

### Before Writing, Confirm You Have:

**Characters:**
- [ ] Main character with contradictions and specifics
- [ ] Distinct voices for each character
- [ ] Clear relationships/dynamics
- [ ] Characters feel real and human

**Setting/Atmosphere:**
- [ ] Specific, concrete details
- [ ] Clear dominant mood
- [ ] Sensory details planned
- [ ] Setting serves emotional purpose

**Structure:**
- [ ] Clear structural type chosen
- [ ] Beginning, middle, end identified
- [ ] Key scenes listed
- [ ] Emotional arc planned

**Dialogue:**
- [ ] Character voices distinct
- [ ] Subtext planned for key conversations
- [ ] Test: Can tell characters apart without tags?

**Theme:**
- [ ] Universal emotion/experience identified
- [ ] Story explores specific question
- [ ] Scenes that explore theme planned

**Mood:**
- [ ] Dominant mood consistent
- [ ] Sensory details support mood
- [ ] Opening and closing moods resonate

### Completing this worksheet means:
- You have a plan
- You know your characters
- You know your structure
- You're ready to write
